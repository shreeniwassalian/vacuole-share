Frequently asked questions

What is an Amazon affiliate link? ▼

An affiliate link includes your unique tracking ID. When someone clicks your link and buys something on Amazon within 24 hours, you earn a commission — usually 1% to 9% depending on the product category.

Where do I find my Amazon Associates tracking ID? ▼

Log in to affiliate-program.amazon.in, go to your account dashboard, and look for "Manage Tracking IDs". Your default tracking ID is your Store ID followed by "-21", for example "yourname-21".

Does this work for all Amazon India products? ▼

Yes — this tool works for any amazon.in product URL. Just make sure the URL contains "/dp/" followed by the product ID, which is the standard format for all Amazon product pages.

Is this tool free? ▼

Completely free, forever. No account needed, no limits on how many links you generate.
