Frequently asked questions

Is my data saved anywhere? ▼

No. Everything you enter stays in your browser only. Nothing is sent to any server. When you close the page, the data is gone. Your wishlist is completely private.

Can I add more than 10 items? ▼

Yes — you can add as many items as you need. Just keep clicking "Add another item" and the calculator will include all of them in the total.

What currency does this use? ▼

The tool displays values in Indian Rupees (₹) by default. You can use any currency — just enter the prices in your local currency and interpret the totals accordingly.

How is the savings timeline calculated? ▼

The savings timeline shows how much of your wishlist total you could afford after 1, 3, and 6 months of saving at your entered monthly budget. It assumes you save that amount consistently each month.
