Frequently asked questions

Is the free version really free forever? ▼

Yes! The free version includes all the essential features and will always be free. You only pay if you want advanced features like unlimited items and cross-device sync.

How do price drop alerts work? ▼

With Vacuole Pro, we monitor prices of items in your wishlist every day. When a product goes on sale or drops in price, we send you an email notification so you never miss a deal.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Absolutely! You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time with just one click. No questions asked, no hidden fees. Your free account will continue to work as before.

Does this work outside India? ▼

Yes! Vacuole works on shopping sites worldwide. While our pricing is in Indian Rupees, the extension works on any website with product links, regardless of location.
