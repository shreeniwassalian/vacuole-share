Refund Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

The short version

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all Pro subscriptions. If you're not happy within 7 days of purchase, email us and we'll refund you — no questions asked.

1. Pro Subscription Pricing

Subscriptions renew automatically. You'll receive a reminder email before renewal.

2. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not satisfied with Vacuole Pro for any reason, you can request a full refund within 7 days of your purchase date.

To request a refund:

1

Email support@getvacuole.com within 7 days of purchase

2

Use the subject line "Refund Request" and include your account email

3

We'll process your refund within 5–7 business days

Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

3. After the 7-Day Window

Refunds are not available after the 7-day window has passed. However:

  • You keep full Pro access until the end of your current billing period
  • You can cancel at any time to prevent future charges
  • We do not offer partial refunds for unused time within a billing period

4. Cancellation

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time — there are no cancellation fees.

  • Cancel from your account settings in the extension
  • After cancelling, you keep Pro features until the end of your paid period
  • Your account reverts to the Free plan automatically after expiry
  • Your saved wishlist data is retained — you are not charged for any new period

5. Renewal Refunds

If your subscription renews and you did not intend to renew, please contact us within 48 hours of the charge at support@getvacuole.com. We'll evaluate these on a case-by-case basis and aim to resolve them quickly.

6. Disputes

If you have a billing issue or dispute, please reach out to us first — we aim to resolve all concerns within 48 hours.

  • General & billing: support@getvacuole.com
  • Payment-specific issues: billing@getvacuole.com

We'd much rather resolve things directly than through a payment dispute. If we can't reach an agreement, disputes are governed by the laws of India with jurisdiction in Pune, Maharashtra.
