Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

The short version

We collect only what we need to run Vacuole. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Vacuole:

Data typeWhat it includesWhy
Account info Email address To create and identify your account
Wishlist data Product titles, URLs, prices, images you save To power your wishlist
Collections & notes Collection names, colours, item notes To organise your wishlist
Price history Historical prices of saved items To detect price drops and send alerts
Usage data When items were saved To show date saved in your list

We do not collect your browsing history, track which sites you visit, or read any page content beyond what you explicitly save.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Vacuole service
  • To send price drop email alerts when a saved item's price decreases (Pro feature — you can disable these)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the service

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase, a managed PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure. Supabase provides:

  • Encryption at rest (AES-256)
  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Row-level security so only you can access your data

Your password is never stored in plain text. Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.

4. Amazon Affiliate Disclosure

Affiliate Disclosure

Vacuole is a participant in the Amazon Associates Programme. When you save an Amazon product, the saved URL may include our affiliate tag (vacuole-21).

If you purchase a product through an Amazon link saved in Vacuole, we may earn a small commission from Amazon. This does not affect the price you pay — Amazon products are priced the same regardless of how you arrive at the page.

This commission helps fund the development and maintenance of Vacuole.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

The Vacuole Chrome extension uses chrome.storage.local to store your session token locally on your device. This is not a browser cookie and is not accessible to websites you visit.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics on the extension or this website.

6. Sharing Your Data

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in these limited cases:

  • Supabase — our database infrastructure provider, who processes data on our behalf
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or government authority

Shared wishlists (Pro feature) display only the items and collection names you choose to share — never your email address.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — view all the data we hold about you
  • Export — download your wishlist as a PDF or spreadsheet (Pro feature)
  • Delete — delete your account and all associated data at any time from the extension settings. Data is removed from our systems within 30 days.
  • Opt out of emails — unsubscribe from price alert emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@getvacuole.com

8. Children's Privacy

Vacuole is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date when we do. For significant changes, we'll notify you by email. Continued use of Vacuole after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at support@getvacuole.com

We're based in Pune, Maharashtra, India.
